### Classic Tomato Soup `br15`
*Silky smooth tomato soup with a touch of cream. The perfect partner for a grilled cheese sandwich.*

**Time:** 30 min · **Servings:** 4 · **Difficulty:** easy
**Tags:** lunch, dinner, easy, comfort-food, one-pot, vegetarian

#### Ingredients
- Canned crushed tomatoes — 28 oz can
- Onion — 1, diced
- Garlic — 3 cloves, minced
- Chicken broth — 1 cup
- Heavy cream — 1/4 cup
- Butter — 2 tbsp
- Sugar — 1 tsp
- Basil — 1 tsp dried

#### Steps
1. Melt butter in a pot over medium heat. Sauté onion until soft, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook 1 minute.
2. Add crushed tomatoes, chicken broth, sugar, and basil. Simmer 15 minutes.
3. Blend soup until smooth using an immersion blender or regular blender.
4. Stir in heavy cream, season with salt and pepper, and serve warm.

### Broccoli Cheddar Soup `br17`
*A thick, velvety soup loaded with broccoli and sharp cheddar. Like the restaurant version, but homemade.*

**Time:** 30 min · **Servings:** 4 · **Difficulty:** easy
**Tags:** lunch, dinner, easy, comfort-food, one-pot, vegetarian

#### Ingredients
- Broccoli florets — 4 cups
- Sharp cheddar cheese — 2 cups, shredded
- Chicken broth — 3 cups
- Milk — 1 cup
- Onion — 1, diced
- Butter — 3 tbsp
- All-purpose flour — 3 tbsp
- Garlic — 2 cloves, minced

#### Steps
1. Melt butter in a large pot. Sauté onion and garlic until soft, about 4 minutes.
2. Stir in flour and cook 1 minute to form a roux. Gradually whisk in broth and milk.
3. Add broccoli and simmer 15 minutes until tender. Mash some broccoli against the side for thickness.
4. Stir in cheddar cheese until melted and smooth. Season with salt and pepper.

### Buddha Bowl `br31`
*A nourishing bowl of roasted sweet potato, chickpeas, greens, avocado, and tahini dressing. Colorful and wholesome.*

**Time:** 35 min · **Servings:** 2 · **Difficulty:** easy
**Tags:** lunch, dinner, healthy, vegetarian, easy, meal-prep

#### Ingredients
- Sweet potato — 1 large, cubed

- Mixed greens — 2 cups
- Avocado — 1, sliced
- Quinoa — 1 cup, cooked
- Tahini — 2 tbsp

### Smashed Cucumber Salad `br56`
*A refreshing Chinese-inspired salad with smashed cucumbers, garlic, chilli, and a tangy sesame-rice vinegar dressing. Done in 10 minutes.*

**Time:** 10 min · **Servings:** 2 · **Difficulty:** easy
**Tags:** lunch, snack, quick, easy, healthy, vegetarian, vegan, dairy-free, gluten-free, salad

#### Ingredients
- Cucumbers — 2 large (or 4 mini)
- Garlic — 2 cloves, minced
- Rice vinegar — 2 tbsp
- Sesame oil — 1 tbsp
- Tamari — 1 tbsp
- Chilli flakes — 1/2 tsp
- Sugar — 1 tsp
- Sesame seeds — 1 tsp, to garnish
- Spring onions — 2, sliced

#### Steps
1. Place cucumbers on a cutting board. Use the flat side of a knife or rolling pin to smash them. Tear into rough pieces.
2. Salt the cucumber, toss, and let sit 5 minutes. Squeeze out any excess water.
3. Whisk together garlic, rice vinegar, sesame oil, tamari, chilli flakes, and sugar.
4. Toss cucumbers with the dressing. Scatter spring onions and sesame seeds on top.

## 40. Cream of Mushroom Soup `br140`
*Velvety mushroom soup with thyme and a splash of sherry.*

**Time:** 40 min · **Servings:** 4 · **Difficulty:** easy
**Tags:** lunch, dinner, easy, vegetarian, comfort-food

### Ingredients
- Mushrooms — 1.5 lbs, sliced
- Onion — 1, diced
- Garlic — 4 cloves
- Butter — 4 tbsp
- Flour — 3 tbsp
- Chicken broth — 4 cups
- Heavy cream — 1/2 cup
- Dry sherry — 2 tbsp
- Fresh thyme — 1 tbsp

### Steps
1. Sauté mushrooms in butter until deeply browned, 12 minutes.
2. Add onion and garlic; cook 5 minutes.
3. Sprinkle flour over; stir 1 minute.
4. Whisk in broth; simmer 15 minutes with thyme.
5. Stir in cream and sherry. Blend half for body.
