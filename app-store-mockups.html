What can I make with chicken breast and broccoli?
🥑
Stir-fry is your best bet — slice the chicken thin, cook with garlic and soy sauce, toss in the broccoli for the last 3 min. Quick, high-protein, and uses both before they expire.
How much protein is in that?
🥑
A 6oz chicken breast has ~50g protein; the broccoli adds 4g — so roughly 54g total per serving, which is excellent for muscle recovery.
