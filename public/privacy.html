Privacy Policy

Effective June 7, 2026 · Last updated June 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pantre (the "App") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. By using Pantre you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Who we are

Pantre is operated by Ryan Elghazzali ("we", "us", or "our"), based in Denver, Colorado, USA. For privacy questions, contact privacy@usepantre.me.

What information we collect

We only collect information you provide or generate by using the App:

  • Account information — your display name, email address, and the sign-in method you chose (Apple, Google, or email + password).
  • Pantry data — items you add (name, category, storage location, quantity, unit, estimated value, expiration date, optional notes), and waste log entries (what action you took on each item).
  • Dietary preferences — any diet tags you select during onboarding (e.g. vegetarian, dairy-free). These are preferences, not health information.
  • Subscription status — whether you are on the Free or Pro tier, plus daily Avo chat usage counters used to enforce limits.
  • Avo AI conversations — when you opt in to Avo AI, your messages (and a short summary of your current pantry contents) are sent to our AI provider for the purpose of generating a response. Conversations are retained on your device for the current chat session only and are cleared when you sign out.
  • Receipt images — if you use the receipt-scan feature, the photo is sent to our AI provider for text extraction. The image is processed in real time and is not stored by Pantre after the parsed list is returned to you.
  • Streak and activity — your current streak count and last-active date, used to power the streak feature and engagement notifications.
  • Device permissions you grant — camera (barcode + receipt scanning) and notifications. These are handled by your device's operating system; Pantre never sees raw camera frames or your notification token outside its intended use.

We do not collect: precise location, contacts, browsing history, advertising IDs, biometric data, financial account numbers, or anything else not listed above. Pantre contains no third-party advertising trackers or analytics SDKs.

How we use your information

  • To operate the core features of the App (pantry tracking, waste logs, recipe suggestions, meal planning).
  • To sync your data across your devices via our backend host (so you don't lose your pantry when you switch phones).
  • To deliver expiration reminders and engagement notifications you've opted into.
  • To answer your messages to Avo AI when you've turned that feature on.
  • To enforce subscription limits (free-tier item caps, daily Avo chat caps).
  • To send you transactional emails — sign-up confirmation, password reset, account deletion confirmation.
  • To investigate abuse, troubleshoot bugs, and protect the security of the App.

We do not use your data to train AI models, and we do not sell or rent it to anyone.

Third-party services (sub-processors)

Pantre relies on a small number of trusted third parties to operate. Each one only receives the data necessary for its role, and each has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase Inc. — hosts your account, pantry, and waste log data on encrypted infrastructure in the United States.
  • Anthropic, PBC — powers Avo AI chat (when you opt in) and receipt OCR. Your chat messages, current pantry snapshot, and receipt photos are sent to Anthropic only at the moment of the request, and only to generate a response. Anthropic's API policy states inputs are not used to train their models by default.
  • Resend, Inc. — delivers transactional emails (welcome, password reset, account deletion confirmation).
  • Apple Inc. — handles Apple Sign-In and (when published) App Store subscription processing. We never receive your Apple ID password or full payment details.
  • Google LLC — handles Google Sign-In. We never receive your Google password.
  • USDA FoodData Central — a public US government dataset we read for food shelf-life lookups. No data flows from you to USDA; we only read their public information.
  • Instacart Inc. — the "Order on Instacart" button in the Plan tab is an affiliate link. Tapping it opens Instacart's website with a search query of your shopping list items. We may receive a small commission if you make a purchase through that link. Once you are on Instacart, you are subject to Instacart's own privacy policy.

If we add or remove a sub-processor in the future, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above.

How long we keep your data

  • We keep your account and pantry data as long as your account is active.
  • If you delete your account from Settings → Account → Delete Account, your pantry items, waste logs, and profile are deleted from our backend within 30 days. Backup copies may persist on Supabase's infrastructure for a short additional period before being overwritten.
  • Avo AI conversations are kept on your device only and are cleared when you sign out.
  • Receipt images are processed in real time and never stored.

Your privacy rights

Regardless of where you live, you can:

  • Access and edit your data from inside the App (Settings, Pantry, Plan, etc.).
  • Export all your data as JSON from Settings → Download My Data.
  • Permanently delete your account from Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  • Email privacy@usepantre.me with any privacy question or request.

If you live in California (CCPA / CPRA): you have the right to know what personal information we have collected about you, to delete it, to correct it, to opt out of "sale" or "sharing" (we do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising), and to non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

If you live in Colorado (Colorado Privacy Act): you have the right to access, correct, delete, and port your personal data. We do not sell your data, do not engage in targeted advertising, and do not profile you in ways that produce legal or similarly significant effects. If we ever begin any of those activities, we will update this policy and provide an opt-out mechanism.

If you live in the European Union or United Kingdom (GDPR / UK GDPR): our legal basis for processing your data is your consent (which you provide by creating an account and, separately, by opting in to Avo AI), and the performance of our contract with you (operating the App). You have the right to access, correct, delete, port, and restrict processing of your data, and to object to processing. You may also lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@usepantre.me. We will respond within 30 days.

Security

We use HTTPS for all data in transit and rely on Supabase's encryption-at-rest for stored data. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If a data breach affects your information, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law — including, for Colorado residents, the 30-day notification requirement under C.R.S. § 6-1-716.

Children

Pantre is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please email privacy@usepantre.me and we will delete it promptly.

International transfers

Pantre is operated from the United States and our sub-processors store data primarily in the United States. If you access Pantre from outside the US, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the "Last updated" date at the top of the page, and material changes will be announced in the App. Continued use of Pantre after a change means you accept the updated policy.
