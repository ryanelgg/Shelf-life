Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pantre ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information: Name, email address, and authentication credentials when you sign up.
  • Pantry data: Food items you add, expiration dates, quantities, locations, and associated metadata.
  • Preferences: Dietary restrictions, household size, and other personalization choices.
  • Communications: Messages you send to Avo (our AI assistant) and support requests.

Information collected automatically

  • Device information: Device type, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage data: Features used, screens viewed, and interactions within the app (collected via PostHog analytics).
  • Crash and error logs: Diagnostic information when the app crashes or encounters errors (collected via Sentry).

Information from third parties

If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address from those providers, subject to your privacy settings with them.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • To personalize recipe recommendations and meal plans
  • To send transactional emails (confirmation codes, account notifications)
  • To respond to your messages and Avo chat queries
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. AI Processing (Avo)

When you chat with Avo, your messages and limited pantry context are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. We do not use your conversations to train AI models. You can decline AI processing during onboarding or in Settings — Avo will be unavailable but other Pantre features continue to work.

4. Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the Service (Supabase for storage, Resend for email, Anthropic for AI, PostHog for analytics, Sentry for diagnostics, RevenueCat for subscriptions).
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect rights, property, or safety.
  • Business successors in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account, which permanently removes your data within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your information (via Settings → Delete Account)
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@usepantre.me.

7. Children's Privacy

Pantre is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no electronic transmission or storage system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Users

Pantre is operated in the United States. If you use the Service from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the U.S. By using Pantre, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will be communicated through the app or via email.
